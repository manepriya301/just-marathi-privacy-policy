Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 3, 2026

Designed for people of all ages — this app aims to be friendly to children, teens, adults, and seniors. Below you'll find short summaries in plain language and a kid-friendly version, followed by the full policy.

Plain-language summary

Short version: Just-Marathi collects only the information it needs to run the app and make it better. We do not sell your personal information. We keep most data on your device. If we share data with service providers (for example, for analytics or crash reporting), they are contractually bound to protect it.

Main points:

  • The app stores most things locally on your device.
  • We collect device and usage info (like crash reports) to improve the app.
  • We ask for permission before accessing things like your camera or storage.
  • This policy is for everyone — including children — and there's a kid-friendly explanation below.

Parent / guardian summary

If your child uses this app: we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) without verifiable parental consent. Full policy — details

1. What we collect

We collect three main types of information:

  • Information you give us: If the app offers registration, profile fields, or you contact support, you may provide name, email, or messages. This is stored only to provide the requested feature.
  • Device and usage data: Device model, OS version, unique (platform) identifiers, session times, feature usage, and crash logs. This helps us diagnose and fix problems and improve the app.
  • Permissions-based data: If you allow camera, storage, or other permissions, the app can access the corresponding data. We request permissions only when necessary for a feature.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and improve app features and performance.
  • To analyze crashes and fix bugs.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations or to protect users' safety and the app's rights.

3. Where data is stored

Most user content and settings are stored locally on your device. If we use remote services (for example cloud backups, analytics, or crash reporting), relevant data may be transmitted to those providers. We minimize what is sent and avoid sending identifiable personal details unless you explicitly provide them.

4. Sharing and third parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (analytics, hosting, crash-reporting) who are contractually restricted from using the data for other purposes.
  • Law enforcement or government bodies if required by law.

5. Cookies and local storage

As a native mobile app, the product mainly uses platform storage rather than browser cookies. If any web views are used, web-based storage/cookies may apply within those web views.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system is 100% secure — please consider this before sharing highly sensitive information.

7. Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the app and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at https://forms.gle/xaYNNBwHoG9XPJN28; we will remove data from active systems unless law requires retention.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, rights may include accessing, correcting, deleting, or exporting personal information. Residents of regions with specific privacy laws (for example, GDPR in the EEA, or CCPA/CPRA in California) have additional rights described in those laws. To exercise rights, contact the developer using the details below.

10. International transfers

Data may be processed or stored in countries different from your residence. By using the app, you consent to transfer of data to such locations. Where required by law, we implement appropriate safeguards.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If changes are material, we will provide notice in the app or by other means and update the effective date at the top.
